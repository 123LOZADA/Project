INTRODUCING TO BLINK

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, debuting on August 8, 2016. The group has four members Jisoo, Jennie, Rose, and Lisa known for their powerful performances and global influence. Combining beauty and strength, BLACKPINK has released hit songs like "DDU-DU DDU-DU", "Kill This Love", and "Pink Venom". They've broken numerous records, performed at Coachella and collaborated with global artists. Their fandom "BLINK" supports them worldwide, making BLACKPINK one of the most successful and influential K-pop groups in history.
